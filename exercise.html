Lung Improvement Exercises

1. Diaphragmatic Breathing

Strengthens diaphragm and improves oxygen exchange.

2. Pursed Lip Breathing

Helps in better air release from lungs.

3. Rib Stretching

Improves chest flexibility.

4. Controlled Coughing

Clears lungs and improves airflow.

5. Walking

Enhances cardiovascular endurance.

6. Cycling

Boosts lung stamina.

7. Yoga (Pranayama)

Increases breathing efficiency.
